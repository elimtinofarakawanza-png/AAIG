Air Zimbabwe

Air Zimbabwe is the national airline of Zimbabwe and a founding member of AAIG. Operating from Harare International Airport (HRE), the airline represents Zimbabwean heritage, national pride, and a long history of African aviation.

AerCYMRU

AerCYMRU is the lead airline of AAIG, representing Wales with modern operations, strong branding, and a growing international network.

Fastjet MAX

Fastjet MAX is AAIG’s low‑cost carrier, offering affordable travel across Africa with a focus on efficiency and accessibility.

Arabic Airways

Arabic Airways connects the Middle East and Africa through AAIG, providing premium service and strong regional connectivity.

FlyAmerican

FlyAmerican is AAIG’s North American partner airline, expanding the alliance’s reach across the United States and beyond.

About AAIG

The Air Africa International Group (AAIG) is a global airline alliance connecting Africa, Europe, the Middle East, and North America through its member airlines.
