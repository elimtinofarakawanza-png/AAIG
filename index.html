About AAIG

The Air Africa International Group (AAIG) is a multinational airline alliance founded on the belief that aviation can unite cultures, strengthen economies, and bring the world closer together. Born in Zimbabwe through the leadership of Air Zimbabwe, AAIG has grown into a global network spanning Africa, Europe, the Middle East, and North America.

AAIG is built on cooperation, innovation, and regional pride. Each member airline maintains its own identity, culture, and operational style — yet all share a unified commitment to excellence, safety, and global connectivity. From the heart of Harare to the skies above Dallas, Cardiff, and Abu Dhabi, AAIG represents a new era of aviation where nations collaborate rather than compete.

Our mission is simple: to create a seamless travel experience across continents while empowering each airline to grow within its region. Whether it’s Fastjet MAX expanding African routes, AerCYMRU connecting Wales to the world, Arabic Airways bridging Africa and the Gulf, or FlyAmerican representing AAIG in the United States — every member plays a vital role in shaping the alliance’s future.

Member Airlines

  • Air Zimbabwe — Founding Member (Hub: Harare HRE)
  • AerCYMRU — Second Member (Hub: Cardiff CWL)
  • Fastjet MAX — Third Member (Hub: Harare HRE)
  • Arabic Airways — Fourth Member (Hub: Abu Dhabi AUH)
  • FlyAmerican — Fifth Member (Hub: Dallas–Fort Worth DFW)

Our Mission

AAIG aims to build a unified aviation group where each airline keeps its identity while gaining the strength and reach of a global alliance. We stand for cooperation, growth, regional pride, and international connectivity.
